Our Story

Authentic Thai Healing Expertise

Thai To Relax was founded by Jessica, who brings over 25 years of expertise in traditional Thai therapeutic massage. Originally from Bangkok, Jessica spent 10 years as a spa therapist at the prestigious Four Seasons Hotel before owning and operating a spa at Baiyoke Hotel for 5 years. In 2000, she relocated to the UK with a mission to bring genuine Thai healing techniques to those seeking real therapeutic outcomes.

Since 2010, Jessica has been running Thai to Relax in Ossett, Wakefield, building a loyal following over 15 years. Unlike typical relaxation spas, our focus is on treating genuine medical conditions—from chronic pain and sciatica to sports injuries and frozen shoulder. Specialising in traditional sen work (pressure point therapy), Jessica has helped countless clients, including rugby and football players, achieve recovery and peak performance. Our clients have cancelled medical procedures in favour of regular treatments with us, a testament to the effectiveness of authentic Thai therapeutic techniques.

Now expanding to Leeds city centre, we're bringing this trusted expertise to a wider community. Every therapist on our team has a minimum of 5 years' experience, carefully selected by Jessica to maintain our high standards. We maintain five-star cleanliness standards and a professional, no-nonsense approach to therapeutic care. Our track record speaks for itself—we deliver measurable results that help people return to the activities they love.

My passion is helping people recover through authentic Thai healing techniques. Jessica Owner
